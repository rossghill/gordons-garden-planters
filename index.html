Welcome to Gordon's Garden Planters.

We are a local business based just outside the village of Cleish in South Kinross-shire and can offer a local delivery service if required.

I have always enjoyed woodworking so after my retirement in 2021, I got a garden workshop built and started designing and making garden planters to keep me occupied with a hobby I very much enjoy.

Maureen, my wife, takes care of the marketing and sales side of things for me. If you have any questions or requests please contact her directly and she will assist you.

There are a selection of sizes and designs that can be viewed on the website along with prices for each. I can also offer a "made to measure" service to suit customer requirements if different sizes or designs are preferred.

We can also make window boxes and fence planters for those with smaller gardens or who live in flats.
